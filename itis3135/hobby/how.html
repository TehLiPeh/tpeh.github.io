How I Conquer

In battles, I have many strategies. Here’s an example. My infantry will build a shield wall and take all incoming arrows from the enemy’s archers. My opponent’s infantry and cavalry will charge towards the shield wall. My archers will hide behind my infantries and fire towards incoming attacks. I will lead my flankers behind the enemy’s archers and attack them from a distance. Then my mighty cavalry will charge from the side of the shield wall, destroying anything along the path.
