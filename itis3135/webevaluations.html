Website Evaluations

Good Website: Riot Games

    CRAP Evaluation

  • Contrast: Everything on this page is readible abd it pleases the eyes of viewers. The color choices on this page is great. The white fonts goes well with the black background and the quality of each pictures are high.
  • Repitition: This website have sections and each sections have the same font style and color. Each section has a big title followed by images.
  • Alignment: This website's alignment is great. Each pictures are placed in a box and they align with other boxes perfectly.
  • Proximity: The proximity on this website is great. Each boxes and images are not too far and not too close.

Bad Website: MGBD Parts & Service

    CRAP Evaluation

  • Contrast: There are too many colors on this website. Some texts on this website is hard to read. For example, the developer decided to use red fonts on the white/gray background. The images are also low quality.
  • Repitition: The repitition on this website is poorly executed. There are too many green toy car on the left and each label confuses the viewers. Some label is bigger than others.
  • Alignment: The alignment on this website is terrible. Each boxes on this website is out of place. Just try to look at the left side of the website.
  • Proximity: Some boxes overlap another box and some needs more space.
